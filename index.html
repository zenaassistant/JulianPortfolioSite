About

The Clinician Behind the Care

Julian's path to psychiatry wasn't linear. After careers in business and solar energy — work that sharpened his eye for systems, operations, and human behavior — he made a deliberate turn toward nursing, driven by a conviction that mental health care needed deeper reform.

He earned his BSN cum laude from CSUN, his BA in Psychology from Vanguard University, and his Master's (PMHNP-BC) cum laude from Western University of Health Sciences in 2025. For over four years he has worked under Dr. Steven Mandel at Ketamine Clinics Los Angeles — one of the country's highest-volume IV ketamine programs — administering up to 10 infusions per day with a consistent record of patient safety.

"Ketamine opens a neuroplastic window — a moment of heightened neural flexibility. My role is to help patients step through it toward lasting change."

Patient History & Pre-Visit Intelligence

A locally hosted system on Mac mini that compiles patient records, session notes, and conversation histories into structured, evolving patient files — organized by a local LLM (Ollama/Mistral). Before each visit, it surfaces a concise summary of the patient's history, progress, and prior interventions. No cloud. No data leaving the device.

Goal: Support every visit with full context · Improve clinical decisions
